How the Process Works

1. Upload your file
Submit your STL or OBJ file using the form below. Include any notes about size, color, quantity, or deadlines.

2. File review
Your model is reviewed for printability, scale, material considerations, and estimated print time.

3. Quote sent
You’ll receive an email with pricing, timeline, and payment instructions.

4. Payment
Payment is accepted via PayPal, Venmo, or Zelle after the quote is approved.

5. Printing & delivery
Once paid, your order is printed and prepared for delivery or pickup.
